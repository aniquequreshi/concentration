Concentration Form (Accounting Department)

Instructions

  • This Concentration Form is a Planning Tool. Its purpose is to help you plan out your Accounting Major courses till you graduate.
  • You should budget at least 30 to 60 minutes to complete this form. This form must be completed before you meet with me.
  • You will need transcripts from all schools you have attended, including Queens College. You will also need a copy of your "transfer credit evaluation" form. If your credits were automatically evaluated, you can get this information from CUNYfirst. Unofficial transcripts are fine.
  • Once you have all your transcripts, start completing the form below. Remember that this is a planning tool. Since you are planning for the future, you will not always know exactly when you will be able to take a particular course. In this case, use your best guess or estimate. It does not need to be perfect. It is OK if things change in the future.
  • Once you have completed the Concentration Form, you will be able to print it out. Please print TWO copies. Sign and Date each copy.
  • Print a copy of the Major Declaration Form from the Queens College Registrar website.
  • Bring the following when meeting with me:
    1. TWO Signed and Dated printed copies of your Concentration Form
    2. Copies of transcripts from all previous schools, as well as Queens College. Unofficial copies are fine.
    3. Fully completed Major Declaration Form.
